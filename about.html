Humble Beginnings

This school started in 1998 and named after Mr.Daud Sahab who donates the land. At present about 350 children are receiving in this school under the supervision of 18 teachers. Over the past few years this school has achieved the status of Senior Secondary School. For this I thank everyone on belief of daudia education welfare society and school arrangement committee and all the teacher and children. Till now 1000 children have received the diploma of takmeele Quran Kareem On this occasion thank all of you took out your precious time and the committee also thank you all for coming. Beside worldly education is necessary for the fast recing of life. keeping these in mind the registered daudia welfare society ramganjmandi has set up such an organisation in which training can be given to 2000 children from class LKG to 12th.
